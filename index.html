My name is Yanxia. I love Graphic Design.

We are not alone in this chaotic and disturbing year of the pandemic, we are all united to face and overcome the emotions of fear and insecurity. I modelled the text with Spline and then I edited the 3D lettering with Photomosh, to overlay it over a picture of a handshake in the background. The handshake was chosen as a sign of solidarity, showing my determination to overcome difficulties caused by the pandemic, but thriving together with my family.

This one illustrates my anxiety and restlessness at the beginning of the pandemic. I used barrel blur and solarize effects on Photomosh to express my emotion through my design. The golden mask added a more sophisticated element to the imagery mood.
