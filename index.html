Assignment 1

We are not alone in this chaotic and disturbing year of the pandemic, we are all united to face and overcome the emotions of fear and insecurity. I modelled the text with Spline and then I edited the 3D lettering with Photomosh, to overlay it over a picture of a handshake in the background. The handshake was chosen as a sign of solidarity, showing my determination to overcome difficulties caused by the pandemic, but thriving together with my family.

This one illustrates my anxiety and restlessness at the beginning of the pandemic. I used barrel blur and solarize effects on Photomosh to express my emotion my design. The golden mask added a more sophisticated element to the imagery mood.

Assignment 2: P5 Code Sketches

For the assignment 2, I had to build 21 iterations using a JS framework called P5.

It wasn't easy since I don't have any experience with programming, so to catch up with everything by watching the programming videos on Youtube, and also reading the documentation, it felt a bit overwhelming to me.

Although it wasn't an easy process, I was able to make some code watching the tutorials and also exploring a few websites where I could have access to step-by-step learning.

This helped me to create a few animations and, also, some mouse interactions that would trigger the animations. It's a broad new field to invest your time, but if you're willing to add code to your graphic design skills, P5 is a good start.
